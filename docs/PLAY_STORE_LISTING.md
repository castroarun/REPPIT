## Full Description (max 4000 characters)

### Plain Text Version (Copy this directly to Play Store):

```
REPPIT - Your Personal Strength Training Coach

Transform your workout routine with REPPIT, the intelligent strength tracking app that automatically measures your fitness level and guides your progression.

AUTOMATIC LEVEL DETECTION
No more guessing where you stand. REPPIT calculates your strength level (Beginner, Novice, Intermediate, or Advanced) for each exercise based on your actual performance relative to your body weight. Log your lifts and watch your levels update in real-time.

SMART PROGRESSION SYSTEM
Get personalized weight and rep targets for every session. The app analyzes your recent performance and tells you exactly when to add weight and when to focus on building reps.

PR TRACKING & CELEBRATIONS
Never miss a personal record! REPPIT automatically detects when you hit new PRs and celebrates your achievements with motivational messages.

INTELLIGENT REST TIMER
- Full-screen countdown with set context
- Customizable presets (2:30 to 5:00)
- Per-exercise memory for your preferred rest times
- Sound and vibration alerts
- Keep screen awake during workouts

COMPREHENSIVE PROGRESS TRACKING
- Visual muscle heatmap showing strength balance
- Body part analysis to identify weak areas
- Workout history for all 23 exercises
- Achievement badges for milestones

23 EXERCISES ACROSS 5 BODY PARTS
Complete coverage including:
- Chest: Bench Press, Incline Press, Dumbbell Fly, and more
- Back: Deadlift, Rows, Pull-ups, Lat Pulldown
- Shoulders: Overhead Press, Lateral Raises
- Legs: Squat, Leg Press, Romanian Deadlift
- Arms: Curls, Tricep exercises

BUILT-IN COACHING FEATURES
- Dynamic warm-up calculator
- Level-specific form tips
- Daily motivational quotes (110+ science-backed messages)
- Body part imbalance detection
- AI coach tips based on your progress

MULTI-PROFILE SUPPORT
Create up to 5 profiles - perfect for sharing with family members or training partners. Each profile tracks independent progress and preferences.

DARK MODE
Easy on the eyes during early morning or late night sessions.

UNIT FLEXIBILITY
Switch between kg and lbs anytime - all conversions happen automatically.

PRIVACY FIRST
All your data stays on your device. No account required, no data collection.

Perfect for:
- Gym beginners wanting structured guidance
- Intermediate lifters tracking progression
- Advanced athletes monitoring performance
- Anyone serious about getting stronger

Download REPPIT today and take your strength training to the next level!
```
